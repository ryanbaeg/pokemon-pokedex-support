Pokédex App

A comprehensive Pokémon guide app providing information on 1025 Pokémon species in 8 languages.

Key Features

  • Detailed information on 1025 Pokémon species
  • Information on Form Changes, Gigantamax, and other transformations
  • Support for 8 languages (Korean, English, Japanese, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Chinese)
