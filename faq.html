Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes, all information is available offline after downloading the app.

Q: How often is the app data updated?

A: Updates are released when new Pokémon are revealed or when information changes.

Q: How do I report data errors?

A: Please send us detailed information about the errors via our contact email, and we will fix them promptly.
